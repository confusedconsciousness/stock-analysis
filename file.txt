## Context

Stock market data can be interesting to analyze and as a further incentive, strong predictive models can have large financial payoff. The amount of financial data on the web is seemingly endless. A large and well structured dataset on a wide array of companies can be hard to come by. Here we provide a dataset with historical stock prices (last 5 years) for all companies currently found on the S&P 500 index.


## Content

All the files have the following columns:

**Date** - in format: yy-mm-dd

**Open** - price of the stock at market open (this is NYSE data so all in USD)

**High** - Highest price reached in the day

**Low Close** - Lowest price reached in the day

**Volume** - Number of shares traded

**Name** - the stock's ticker name
